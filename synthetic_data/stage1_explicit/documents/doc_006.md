Policy POL-SHIP-018 (department priority) stipulates that when item_in_stock equals True, agents must ship_within_48h; however, an exception applies whereby if severe_weather_alert equals True, then allow_delay is permitted.

Under Policy POL-RETURNS-014 (department priority), agents must offer a refund when the customer has a receipt (has_receipt == True) and the purchase was made within 21 days (days_since_purchase <= 21); however, if physical damage is present (physical_damage == True), agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or purchase date.

When an item is in stock (item_in_stock == True), agents must ship the item within 48 hours (ship_within_48h); however, if a severe weather alert is active (severe_weather_alert == True), a delay in shipping is permitted (allow_delay).

Under Policy POL-REFUND-015, designated as situational priority, agents must offer a refund to customers when the customer has a receipt (has_receipt == True) and the purchase was made within 28 days or fewer (days_since_purchase <= 28); however, if physical damage is present (physical_damage == True), agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or purchase date.

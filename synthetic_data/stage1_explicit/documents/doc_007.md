Under Policy POL-RETURNS-019, which is designated as situational priority, agents must offer a refund to customers when the customer has a receipt (has_receipt equals True) and the purchase was made within 28 days or fewer (days_since_purchase is less than or equal to 28); however, if physical damage is present (physical_damage equals True), agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or time since purchase.

Under Policy POL-RETURNS-009 (company priority), agents must offer a refund to customers when has_receipt == True and days_since_purchase <= 14; however, if physical_damage == True, agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or days since purchase.

Under Policy POL-PRIVACY-001, designated as company priority, when any data or information contains personally identifiable information (contains_pii == True), agents shall be required to disclose the presence of such personally identifiable information (disclose_pii).

Policy POL-PRIVACY-006 (department priority) mandates that agents must disclose personally identifiable information (PII) when the data being processed contains PII, as determined by the condition contains_pii == True.

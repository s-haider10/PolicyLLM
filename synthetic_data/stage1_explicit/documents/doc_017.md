When a refund amount is greater than or equal to 400, agents shall be required to verify the identity of the customer, as established under Policy POL-SEC-012 with regulatory priority classification.

Under Policy POL-REFUND-020, which holds regulatory priority, agents must offer a refund to customers when the customer has a receipt (has_receipt == True) and the purchase was made within seven days or fewer (days_since_purchase <= 7); however, if the item exhibits physical damage (physical_damage == True), agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or purchase timeframe.

When a refund amount is greater than or equal to 400, agents shall be required to verify the customer's identity prior to processing the refund, as established under Policy POL-SEC-007 (situational priority).

Under Policy POL-RETURNS-009, designated as company priority, agents must offer a refund to customers when the customer has a receipt (has_receipt == True) and the purchase was made within fourteen days or fewer (days_since_purchase <= 14); however, if physical damage is present (physical_damage == True), agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or purchase date.

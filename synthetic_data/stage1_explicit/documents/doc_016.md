# Stage 1 synthetic document 16

Under Policy POL-RETURNS-009, designated as company priority, agents must offer a refund to customers when the customer has a receipt (has_receipt == True) and the purchase was made within fourteen days or fewer (days_since_purchase <= 14); however, if the item exhibits physical damage (physical_damage == True), agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or purchase date.

Under Policy POL-REFUND-015, designated as situational priority, agents must offer a refund to customers when the customer has a receipt (has_receipt equals True) and the purchase was made within 28 days or fewer (days_since_purchase is less than or equal to 28); however, if physical damage is present (physical_damage equals True), agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or time since purchase.

When a refund amount is greater than or equal to 400, agents shall be required to verify the identity of the requestor, as mandated by Policy POL-SEC-012 under regulatory priority classification.

Policy POL-PRIVACY-006 (department priority) mandates that agents must disclose personally identifiable information (PII) when the data contains_pii equals True.

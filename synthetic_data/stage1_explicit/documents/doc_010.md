Under Policy POL-SHIP-013, designated as company priority, agents must ship items within 48 hours when the item is in stock (item_in_stock == True); however, an exception applies whereby delays are permitted when a severe weather alert is active (severe_weather_alert == True).

Under Policy POL-RETURNS-004, designated as regulatory priority, agents must offer a refund to customers when the customer has a receipt (has_receipt == True) and the purchase was made within seven days or fewer (days_since_purchase <= 7); however, if physical damage is present (physical_damage == True), agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or days since purchase.

When a refund amount is greater than or equal to 400, agents shall be required to verify the identity of the customer, as established under Policy POL-SEC-012 with regulatory priority classification.

Under Policy POL-SHIP-018, designated as department priority, agents must ship items within 48 hours when item_in_stock equals True; however, an exception applies whereby delays are permitted when severe_weather_alert equals True.

Under Policy POL-RETURNS-009, designated as company priority, agents must offer a refund to customers when the customer has a receipt (has_receipt == True) and the purchase was made within fourteen days or fewer (days_since_purchase <= 14); however, if physical damage is present (physical_damage == True), agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or purchase timeframe.

Agents must offer a refund to customers when the customer has a receipt and the number of days since purchase is less than or equal to 14; however, if physical damage is present, agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or days since purchase.

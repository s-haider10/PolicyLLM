Under Policy POL-REFUND-020, which holds regulatory priority, agents must offer a refund to customers when the customer has a receipt (has_receipt == True) and the purchase was made within seven days or fewer (days_since_purchase <= 7); however, if physical damage is present (physical_damage == True), agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or purchase timeframe.

Under Policy POL-PRIVACY-001, designated as company priority, when any data or information contains personally identifiable information (contains_pii equals True), agents shall be required to disclose the presence of such personally identifiable information (disclose_pii).

When processing requests under Policy POL-SEC-017, which is designated as company priority, agents must verify the customer's identity if the refund amount is greater than or equal to 400.

Under Policy POL-RETURNS-014 (department priority), agents must offer a refund when the customer has a receipt (has_receipt == True) and the purchase was made within 21 days (days_since_purchase <= 21); however, if physical damage is present (physical_damage == True), agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or days since purchase.

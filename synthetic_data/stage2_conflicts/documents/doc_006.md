# Stage 2 synthetic document 6

When a refund amount is greater than or equal to 400, agents shall be required to verify the customer's identity, as established under Policy POL-SEC-017 and designated as a company priority.

Under Policy POL-RETURNS-004, designated as regulatory priority, agents must offer a refund to customers when the customer has a receipt (has_receipt == True) and the purchase was made within seven days or fewer (days_since_purchase <= 7); however, if physical damage is present (physical_damage == True), agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or days since purchase.

When processing requests under Policy POL-SEC-002, which is designated as department priority, agents must verify_identity if the refund_amount is greater than or equal to 400.

Under Policy POL-RETURNS-019, which operates as a situational priority policy, agents must offer a refund to customers when has_receipt equals True and days_since_purchase is less than or equal to 28 days; however, if physical_damage equals True, agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or purchase date.

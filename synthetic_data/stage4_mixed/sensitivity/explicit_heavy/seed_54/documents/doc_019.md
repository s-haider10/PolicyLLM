Policy POL-PRIVACY-006 establishes that when any data contains personally identifiable information (contains_pii == True), agents shall be required to disclose the presence of such personally identifiable information (disclose_pii), and this policy is designated as department priority.

Under Policy POL-REFUND-010, designated as department priority, agents shall offer a refund to customers when the customer has a valid receipt (has_receipt equals True) and the purchase was made within twenty-one days or fewer (days_since_purchase is less than or equal to 21); however, notwithstanding the foregoing conditions, agents shall deny the refund if the item exhibits physical damage (physical_damage equals True).

Under Policy POL-REFUND-015 (situational priority), agents must offer a refund to customers when has_receipt equals True and days_since_purchase is less than or equal to 28 days; however, if physical_damage equals True, agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or purchase date.

Under Policy POL-PRIVACY-016, designated as regulatory priority, when data contains personally identifiable information (contains_pii == True), agents shall be required to disclose the presence of such personally identifiable information (disclose_pii).

Under Policy POL-REFUND-020, which carries regulatory priority, agents must offer a refund to customers when the customer has a receipt (has_receipt == True) and the purchase was made within seven days or fewer (days_since_purchase <= 7); however, if physical damage is present (physical_damage == True), agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or purchase timeframe.

When a refund request is submitted with a refund_amount greater than or equal to 400, agents must perform verify_identity before processing the refund, as specified under Policy POL-SEC-007 (situational priority).

Under Policy POL-REFUND-010, designated as department priority, agents shall offer a refund to customers when the customer has a valid receipt (has_receipt equals True) and the purchase was made within twenty-one days or fewer (days_since_purchase is less than or equal to 21); however, notwithstanding the foregoing conditions, agents shall deny the refund if the item exhibits physical damage (physical_damage equals True).

Under Policy POL-RETURNS-019, which is designated as situational priority, agents must offer a refund to customers when the customer has a receipt (has_receipt equals True) and the number of days since purchase is less than or equal to 28; however, if physical damage is present (physical_damage equals True), agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or days since purchase.

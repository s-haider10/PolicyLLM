# Stage 4 (explicit) synthetic document 2

Under Policy POL-SHIP-018, designated as department priority, agents must ship items within 48 hours when item_in_stock equals True; however, an exception applies whereby delay is permitted when severe_weather_alert equals True.

Under Policy POL-REFUND-015 (situational priority), agents must offer a refund to customers when has_receipt equals True and days_since_purchase is less than or equal to 28 days; however, if physical_damage equals True, agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or purchase date.

When an item is in stock (item_in_stock == True), agents must ship the item within 48 hours (ship_within_48h); however, if a severe weather alert is active (severe_weather_alert == True), agents are permitted to delay shipment beyond the standard 48-hour requirement.

Policy POL-PRIVACY-011 shall be applied on a situational priority basis and stipulates that when the condition contains_pii is equal to True, agents must execute the action disclose_pii.

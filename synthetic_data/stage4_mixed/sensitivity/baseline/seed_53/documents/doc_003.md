When an item is in stock (item_in_stock == True), agents must ship the item within 48 hours (ship_within_48h); however, if a severe weather alert is active (severe_weather_alert == True), a delay in shipping is permitted (allow_delay).

Under Policy POL-REFUND-015, designated as situational priority, agents must offer a refund to customers when the customer has a receipt (has_receipt equals True) and the purchase was made within twenty-eight days or fewer (days_since_purchase is less than or equal to 28); however, if physical damage is present (physical_damage equals True), agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or time since purchase.

When a refund request is submitted with a refund_amount greater than or equal to 400, agents must perform verify_identity before processing the refund, as specified under Policy POL-SEC-007 (situational priority).

Under Policy POL-REFUND-020, which holds regulatory priority, agents must offer a refund to customers when the customer has a receipt (has_receipt == True) and the purchase was made within seven days or fewer (days_since_purchase <= 7); however, if the item exhibits physical damage (physical_damage == True), agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or purchase date.

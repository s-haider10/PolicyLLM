When a refund amount is greater than or equal to 400, agents shall be required to verify the customer's identity prior to processing the refund, as established under Policy POL-SEC-007 (situational priority).

Under Policy POL-RETURNS-009, designated as company priority, agents must offer a refund to customers when the customer has a receipt (has_receipt == True) and the purchase was made within fourteen days or fewer (days_since_purchase <= 14); however, if physical damage is present (physical_damage == True), agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or purchase date.

Policy POL-PRIVACY-016 (regulatory priority) states that when data contains personally identifiable information (contains_pii equals True), agents shall be required to disclose the presence of personally identifiable information (disclose_pii).

Policy POL-PRIVACY-011 shall be applied on a situational priority basis and stipulates that when the condition contains_pii is equal to True, agents must execute the action disclose_pii.

Under Policy POL-PRIVACY-001, designated as company priority, when any data or information contains personally identifiable information (contains_pii == True), agents shall be required to disclose the presence of such personally identifiable information (disclose_pii).

Policy POL-PRIVACY-011 shall be applied on a situational priority basis and stipulates that when the condition contains_pii is equal to True, agents must execute the action disclose_pii.

Under Policy POL-RETURNS-019, which is designated as situational priority, agents must offer a refund to customers when the customer has a receipt (has_receipt equals True) and the number of days since purchase is less than or equal to 28; however, if physical damage is present (physical_damage equals True), agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or days since purchase.

Under Policy POL-SHIP-013, designated as company priority, agents must ship items within 48 hours when the item is in stock (item_in_stock == True); however, an exception applies whereby delays are permitted when a severe weather alert is active (severe_weather_alert == True).

# Stage 4 (explicit) synthetic document 15

When a refund amount is greater than or equal to 400, agents shall be required to verify the customer's identity, as established under Policy POL-SEC-007 (situational priority).

When an item is in stock (item_in_stock == True), agents must ship the item within 48 hours (ship_within_48h); however, if a severe weather alert is active (severe_weather_alert == True), agents are permitted to delay shipment beyond the standard 48-hour requirement.

Under Policy POL-RETURNS-019, which is designated as situational priority, agents must offer a refund to customers when the customer has a receipt (has_receipt equals True) and the purchase was made within 28 days or fewer (days_since_purchase is less than or equal to 28); however, if physical damage is present (physical_damage equals True), agents must deny the refund regardless of receipt status or time since purchase.

Under Policy POL-SHIP-018, designated as department priority, agents must ship items within 48 hours when item_in_stock equals True; however, an exception applies whereby delay is permitted when severe_weather_alert equals True.

# Customer Support Policy - TechStore

## Return and Refund Policy

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2024
**Policy Owner:** Customer Service Team
**Priority:** Company Policy

### Electronics Returns

Customers may return electronics products under the following conditions:

1. **With Receipt - Full Refund**: If the customer has a valid receipt and the product was purchased within 14 days, they are eligible for a full refund.

2. **With Receipt - Extended Return**: If the customer has a valid receipt but the product was purchased between 15-30 days ago, they are eligible for store credit only.

3. **Without Receipt**: If the customer does not have a receipt, returns are not accepted for electronics products.

### Laptops Specific Policy

For laptop computers specifically:

- Must be returned in original packaging
- All accessories must be included
- Device must be free of physical damage
- Maximum refund amount for laptops is $2000

### Privacy and Security Constraints

**IMPORTANT - Always Apply:**

- NEVER disclose customer personal information (PII) including social security numbers, credit card numbers, or email addresses in responses
- NEVER make unconditional guarantees or promises to customers
- Always verify customer identity before processing refunds over $500
